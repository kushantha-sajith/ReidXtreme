What is ReidXtreme?

IEEE SB of UCSC is organizing a 24-hour competitive programming competition for UCSC undergraduates. The competition is to be held for the second time by IEEE SB of UCSC. In 2020 ReidXtreme was one of the most successful events held by IEEE SB of UCSC.
